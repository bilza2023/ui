# Linear Equations (Exercise 1.1 – Q1)

::intro::
Linear equations are equations of the form ax + b = 0 where a ≠ 0.

::definition::
A linear equation has a constant rate of change and graphs as a straight line.

::example::
Solve: 2x + 3 = 7  
Step 1: Subtract 3 from both sides  
Step 2: Divide by 2  
Answer: x = 2

::image-prompt::
"Graph showing a linear equation y = 2x + 1 intersecting y-axis at 1"

::teacher-note::
Students often confuse linear with quadratic. Reinforce that linear = straight line.
