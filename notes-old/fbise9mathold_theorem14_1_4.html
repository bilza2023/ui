Theorem 14.4 – Similar Triangles ⇒ Proportional Corresponding Sides

Book Statement

If two triangles are similar, the measures of their corresponding sides are proportional.

Key Vocabulary

  • Similar Triangles: Triangles with all corresponding angles equal.
  • Corresponding Sides: Sides opposite equal angles in similar figures.

Summary

Similarity establishes a constant scale factor between the triangles.
